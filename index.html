How to use

1

In Ableton, right-click each MIDI track and export/save as a .mid file. Do this for every track in your project.

2

Drop all your .mid files into the box above. Drag to reorder them — the order here becomes the track order in the final file.

3

Click Merge & Download. You'll get a single Type 1 .mid file you can drag straight into any DAW.

no files are uploaded anywhere — everything runs in your browser
